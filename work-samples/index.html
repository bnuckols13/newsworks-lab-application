Work samples

Three packages, two desks, one engine

Three published western-Pennsylvania stories, each shipped as a live, one-command-reproducible data package. Two ran in Public Source and one in the West Hills Gazette, so the method has cleared two regional desks. Every package recomputes its own numbers with python 03-harness/check.py, offline.

Sample 1 · Public Source · Dec 2025

The Pittsburgh Water Deliberation Record

Two years of board votes coded from the official minutes on four variables. The finding: 228 resolutions, 223 unanimous, zero no votes, more than $871 million authorized, and 156 resolutions that drew no recorded question. A random-sampled peer test put the board's 100% unanimity against 69% at comparable water boards, and nine named human sources carry the claims.

Sample 2 · Public Source · Sept 2025

The Pittsburgh Inclusionary Zoning Record

Six years after the city required affordable units in two neighborhoods, it published no count of what the rule produced. The package builds the count the city never released: what is built, what is under construction, what is merely proposed, and the projects downsized to duck the threshold.

Sample 3 · West Hills Gazette · Oct 2025

The Pittsburgh Airport Terminal Record

Reconstructs the figures the airport authority would not give at its own ribbon-cutting: $1.248 billion in modernization bonds outstanding and $2.505 billion in debt service to 2056, against a terminal built for 13 to 15 million passengers while 2024 demand held at 9.95 million. Neutral cost language throughout.

And the result · Public Source

The observer effect

At the first board meeting after Sample 1 ran, the Pittsburgh Water board asked 40 questions, roughly seven times its 24-month average, and issued a stop-work order. Not "I did a rigorous study" but "the reporting measurably changed a public body." Written third person, the institution as the subject.

The through-line

All three run on the same engine: documented, archived, re-verifiable public-records work, published so a reader can check it rather than trust it. The regional clip and the reproducible package are the same method at two altitudes, and the package is built to hand to a partner newsroom intact.
