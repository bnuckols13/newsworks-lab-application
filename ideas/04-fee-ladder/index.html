The Fee Ladder

On a row house in the Mon Valley, a tax delinquency of two or three hundred dollars turns into a payoff the family cannot make, because the charges to redeem it, all of them lawful, pass the tax itself before the sale is even scheduled. The fee ladder, not the tax, is what puts the house out of reach.

The pitch

The hook

A single praecipe for a writ of scire facias, the routine filing that pushes a delinquent parcel toward tax sale, carries a $450 attorney fee under Allegheny County's Chapter 405. Add ten percent annual interest on every advanced cost and the lien-filing charges recorded by parcel, and on a row-house delinquency of two or three hundred dollars the charges to redeem pass the tax itself before the sale is ever scheduled.

The story

Owner-occupied homes in Wilkinsburg, McKeesport, Duquesne, and Braddock enter the tax-sale pipeline over small delinquencies where accumulated interest, lien costs, and collection fees exceed the base tax owed. Every charge is lawful. The story is the ratio: how often, in the county's lowest-value municipalities, the fee stack clears the tax, and what a three-hundred-dollar shortfall becomes by the time a family is asked to redeem. The reporting measures a mechanism; it alleges no wrongdoing.

Why now

Senate Bill 202 cut Allegheny County's post-sale redemption window from nine months to three, effective for sales on or after September 5, 2023. Families now have a quarter of the time to assemble a payoff the fee ladder has already inflated, and the county's annual sale calendar gives the series a recurring peg.

The reporting plan

Built: the WPRDC lien dataset matches liens to base tax and computes the fee-to-tax ratio by municipality. The build: a Right-to-Know request for Jordan Tax Service's contracted fee schedule and the county's cost-recovery terms; docket pulls from the Department of Court Records to confirm per-parcel charges; owner-occupancy screening against assessment records.

Logistics

The node map runs by municipality, by school-district filer, and by the fee line itself, so the build supports a cadence of short bites, one municipality and one ratio at a time. The reporting has a real reason to leave the building: homeowners on the current sale lists in the four boroughs above.

The close

The county publishes the tax owed on every parcel. It does not publish what the charges to keep the house eventually total. That number is the story, and it is sitting in records anyone can download.

The discipline

The mechanics are lawful, so the story is the fee-to-tax ratio, never illegality. Fee language stays lawful throughout: charges, accrue, exceed, never lost or stolen or stripped. Jordan Tax Service and the taxing bodies are named as documented fee recipients with right of reply; no homeowner or parcel is named until owner-occupancy is verified and the witness gate clears.
